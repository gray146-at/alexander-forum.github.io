Publications

Our Foundation Dossiers and Policy Briefs explore central future topics from an interdisciplinary perspective – with a focus on resilience, the polycrisis, and societal transformation.

We work evidence-based and transparently, publish openly accessible materials, and strengthen understanding of complex systems. Dossiers offer structured analyses; future Policy Briefs will provide compact, action-oriented recommendations.
