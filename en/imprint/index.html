Editorial orientation

The Alexander Forum publishes analyses, dossiers and contextual essays on social and individual resilience, polycrisis, technology, health, psychology, democracy, social cohesion and future-oriented transformation. Its publications are intended to support public reflection, orientation and informed debate.
