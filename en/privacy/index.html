Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains which personal data may be processed when you visit this website or contact us.

Controller

Alexander Forum für Reflexion & Resilienz
Responsible: Dorian Alexander Rammer
Registered location: Vienna, Austria
Email: kontakt@alexanderforum.org

Hosting and technical delivery

This website is provided as a static website via GitHub Pages. When the website is accessed, technically necessary connection data may be processed, such as IP address, time of access, requested file, browser and operating system information. This processing is required to deliver the website, operate it securely and detect technical errors.

Contact

If you contact us by email, the data you provide will be processed to handle your request and possible follow-up questions. Without this data, the request cannot be answered.

Basic statistics with Simple Analytics

We use Simple Analytics for privacy-friendly basic website statistics. According to Simple Analytics, the service works without tracking cookies and without creating personal user profiles. Aggregated usage information is collected to understand which pages are visited and how the website is used in general terms.

Optional extended analytics with Microsoft Clarity

In addition, Microsoft Clarity may be loaded after your consent. Clarity helps us better understand usage patterns, including clicks, scrolling behaviour, sessions and possible display problems. Usage data may be processed and analytics cookies may be set. On this website, Clarity is loaded only if you consent to optional statistics in the banner.

You can reopen and change your decision at any time via the “Privacy settings” link in the footer.

Legal bases

The technical delivery of the website is based on legitimate interests in a secure and functional web presence. Optional extended analytics with Microsoft Clarity are based only on your consent.

Your rights

Within the limits of applicable law, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, objection and withdrawal of consent. You may also lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if technical, legal or organizational circumstances change.
